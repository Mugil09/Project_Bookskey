Rich Dad Poor Dad

By Robert T. Kiyosaki

A personal finance classic that contrasts the mindset and financial strategies of the author's two "dads"—his biological father (Poor Dad) and the father of his best friend (Rich Dad)—to teach lessons on wealth-building, investing, and achieving financial independence.

Atomic Habits

By James Clear

Atomic Habits is a transformative guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones through small, incremental changes. James Clear explains how tiny improvements, made consistently over time, can compound into remarkable results.Drawing from psychology.
